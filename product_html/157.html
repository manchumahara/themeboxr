What Fabulinus?

Fabulinus - is Kindergarten & Kids School psd template cooked using latest technology like bootstrap 4 grid, kids friendly fonts and design.

The theme has 14 Layered PSD file with proper naming of layers and objects.

Fabulinus theme is designed thinking Kindergarten & Kids School, all necessary pages for such school website with kids friendly design that can make any kid school website different from common ones.
